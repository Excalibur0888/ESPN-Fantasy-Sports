Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ESPN Fantasy Sports ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website and mobile application (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to enhance your fantasy sports experience:

  • Account information (username, email, password)
  • Profile data (name, avatar, preferences)
  • Game statistics and performance data
  • Device and usage information
  • Payment details (when applicable)

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our fantasy sports platform:

  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Process transactions and manage leagues
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Analyze platform performance and trends
  • Ensure fair play and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and business partners
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities
  • Other users (with your consent)
  • In connection with a business transaction

Data Protection

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure payment processing
  • Access controls and authentication

Your Rights

As a user of ESPN Fantasy Sports, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Export your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control your privacy settings

Security Measures

We maintain strict security protocols to protect your information, including regular penetration testing, encrypted data storage, and continuous monitoring of our systems. Our dedicated security team works around the clock to prevent unauthorized access and ensure data integrity.

Policy Updates

We regularly review and update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and services. Major changes will be notified via email or app notifications, and the latest version will always be available on our website.
