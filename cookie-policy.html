Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are essential digital tools that enhance your fantasy sports experience. These small data files help us remember your preferences, secure your account, and provide personalized features that make our platform more engaging and user-friendly.

Cookie Categories

We utilize different types of cookies to power various features:

  • Performance cookies: Track platform usage and improve speed
  • Authentication cookies: Keep you securely logged in
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand user behavior
  • Feature cookies: Enable advanced platform functionality

Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies for your current browsing session
  • Persistent cookies: Long-term cookies for remembering your preferences
  • Essential cookies: Required for core platform functionality
  • Third-party cookies: From trusted partners and analytics providers

Cookie Controls

You have full control over your cookie preferences:

  • Cookie settings panel in your account dashboard
  • Browser cookie management tools
  • Individual cookie category toggles
  • Cookie consent management system

Cookie Impact

While you can disable non-essential cookies, this may affect certain features of our fantasy sports platform. Essential cookies will remain active to ensure basic functionality and security. We recommend keeping cookies enabled for the best gaming experience.

Policy Changes

Our Cookie Policy is regularly updated to reflect new features and technologies. We'll notify you of significant changes through our platform and provide easy access to the latest version of our policy.
