Common Questions

Expert answers to your fantasy sports queries

What steps do I need to take to start my fantasy team?
Starting your fantasy journey is straightforward! Download our app, create your account, and select your preferred sport. Our intuitive team builder will guide you through player selection, team naming, and league participation options. You'll be managing your dream team in minutes!
What are the costs associated with playing?
We believe in making fantasy sports accessible to everyone! Our platform is entirely free to download and use. You can create teams, participate in leagues, and enjoy the complete fantasy sports experience without any subscription fees or hidden charges.
How does the point calculation system work?
Our dynamic scoring system reflects real-world sports performance. Each action your players make in actual games earns specific points - from game-winning plays to defensive achievements. Visit the 'League Rules' section in your dashboard for a comprehensive breakdown of point values for each sport.
Can I participate in several leagues simultaneously?
Absolutely! Our platform supports multi-league participation. Whether you prefer competitive public leagues or private competitions with friends, you can manage multiple teams across different leagues. Each league offers unique settings, draft rules, and potential rewards.
What support options are available for technical issues?
We offer comprehensive support through multiple channels. Our expert team is available round-the-clock via live chat in the app, email support, or through our dedicated help center. Most queries receive responses within 2 hours, ensuring you never miss important fantasy sports moments.
